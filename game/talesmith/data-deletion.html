Data deletion for Talesmith

Effective date: August 30, 2025

Talesmith ("we", "our", or "us") built the Talesmith app, available on Google Play. This page explains how you can permanently delete your data we stored.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app:

  • Go to Settings > Delete Account.
  • Enter your email and password to confirm deletion.
  • All data associated with your account will then be permanently deleted from our servers.
